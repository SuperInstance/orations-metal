# orations-metal — Three Perspectives on Load-Bearing Abstractions

Design documents (orations) from the Architect, the Physicist, and Hermes, debating the structural principles behind the OpenConstruct math crates: symplectic geometry, Fibonacci growth, spectral deadbands, and conservation ratios.

## What This Gives You

- **The Architect** — on structural honesty, load-bearing abstractions, and systems that don't lie
- **The Physicist** — on symplectic geometry, Hamiltonian conservation, and why Euler drifts
- **Hermes** — on the messenger's perspective: eigenvalues as communication channels

These orations shaped the design of crates like `symplectic-spin`, `fibonacci-growth`, `spectral-deadband`, and the conservation-ratio framework that runs through the entire OpenConstruct ecosystem.
